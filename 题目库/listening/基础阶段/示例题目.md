# 听力练习 - 基础阶段

## 题目来源
Cambridge IELTS 10, Test 1, Section 1

## 音频文本
**场景**: 电话咨询 - 预订旅游行程

**对话内容**:
- **Agent**: Good morning, Travel First Agency. How can I help you?
- **Customer**: Hello, I'd like to book a holiday to Australia.
- **Agent**: Certainly. When would you like to travel?
- **Customer**: In July, please.
- **Agent**: And how long would you like to stay?
- **Customer**: Two weeks.
- **Agent**: Fine. And what's your name, please?
- **Customer**: It's Sarah Jenkins.
- **Agent**: Could you spell your surname, please?
- **Customer**: J-E-N-K-I-N-S.
- **Agent**: Thank you. And your address?
- **Customer**: 14 Hill Road, London.
- **Agent**: And your phone number?
- **Customer**: 0207 456 7890.
- **Agent**: Right. Now, what type of accommodation would you prefer?
- **Customer**: A hotel, please.
- **Agent**: And would you like breakfast included?
- **Customer**: Yes, please.

## 题目

### Questions 1-5
Complete the form below. Write NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer.

**Holiday Booking Form**

1. Destination: _______________
2. Travel month: _______________
3. Duration: _______________
4. Customer's surname: _______________
5. Address: _______________

### Questions 6-10
Complete the form below. Write NO MORE THAN ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER for each answer.

6. Phone number: _______________
7. Accommodation type: _______________
8. Breakfast included: _______________

## 答案

### 正确答案
1. Australia
2. July
3. Two weeks / 2 weeks
4. Jenkins
5. 14 Hill Road, London
6. 0207 456 7890
7. Hotel
8. Yes

## 详细解析

### 题目1: Destination
- **答案**: Australia
- **解析**: 对话中客户明确说"I'd like to book a holiday to Australia"，所以目的地是Australia。
- **易错点**: 注意拼写，首字母大写。

### 题目2: Travel month
- **答案**: July
- **解析**: 当被问到"When would you like to travel?"时，客户回答"In July, please"。
- **易错点**: 月份首字母需要大写。

### 题目3: Duration
- **答案**: Two weeks / 2 weeks
- **解析**: 客户回答"How long would you like to stay?"时说"Two weeks"。
- **易错点**: 可以写成"Two weeks"或"2 weeks"，但注意题目要求不超过3个词。

### 题目4: Customer's surname
- **答案**: Jenkins
- **解析**: 客户说名字是"Sarah Jenkins"，然后拼写了姓氏"J-E-N-K-I-N-S"，所以surname是Jenkins。
- **易错点**: 注意区分first name (Sarah) 和 surname (Jenkins)。

### 题目5: Address
- **答案**: 14 Hill Road, London
- **解析**: 客户直接提供了完整地址"14 Hill Road, London"。
- **易错点**: 注意拼写，Road首字母大写。

### 题目6: Phone number
- **答案**: 0207 456 7890
- **解析**: 客户明确提供了电话号码。
- **易错点**: 注意数字的准确记录，不要遗漏任何数字。

### 题目7: Accommodation type
- **答案**: Hotel
- **解析**: 当被问到"What type of accommodation would you prefer?"时，客户回答"A hotel, please"。
- **易错点**: 注意单数形式，不要写成hotels。

### 题目8: Breakfast included
- **答案**: Yes
- **解析**: 当被问到"Would you like breakfast included?"时，客户回答"Yes, please"。
- **易错点**: 注意题目要求不超过1个词，所以只写"Yes"即可。

## 学习要点

1. **数字和字母拼写**: 在Section 1中经常需要记录姓名、地址、电话号码等，要特别注意拼写准确性。

2. **同义词替换**: 题目中可能用不同的词表达相同的意思，如"duration"对应"how long"。

3. **注意题目要求**: 本题要求"NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS"或"NO MORE THAN ONE WORD"，超出字数限制会扣分。

4. **首字母大写**: 专有名词（地名、月份、姓名等）需要首字母大写。

## 相关词汇
- destination: 目的地
- accommodation: 住宿
- duration: 持续时间
- surname: 姓氏
- booking: 预订
