# 写作练习 - Task 1 基础阶段

## 题目来源
Cambridge IELTS 10, Test 1, Writing Task 1

## 题目

**The charts below show the percentage of their food budget the average family spent on restaurant meals in different years. The graph shows the number of meals eaten in fast food restaurants and sit-down restaurants.**

**Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.**

Write at least 150 words.

### 图表描述
（注：实际考试中会提供图表，这里用文字描述）

**Pie Chart 1 - 1970年**
- 家庭食物预算中，餐厅用餐占10%
- 家庭烹饪占90%

**Pie Chart 2 - 2000年**
- 家庭食物预算中，餐厅用餐占50%
- 家庭烹饪占50%

**Line Graph - 1970-2000年餐厅用餐次数**
- 快餐店用餐次数：从1970年的20次/年增长到2000年的90次/年
- 正餐厅用餐次数：从1970年的10次/年增长到2000年的50次/年

## 参考范文

The pie charts illustrate the proportion of food budget allocated to restaurant meals in 1970 and 2000, while the line graph shows the number of meals consumed in fast food and sit-down restaurants over the same period.

Looking at the pie charts first, there was a dramatic increase in the percentage of food budget spent on restaurant meals. In 1970, only 10% of the average family's food budget was spent on restaurant meals, with the remaining 90% allocated to home cooking. By 2000, this figure had risen significantly to 50%, indicating that families were spending equal amounts on restaurant meals and home cooking.

Turning to the line graph, the number of meals eaten in both types of restaurants increased substantially over the 30-year period. Fast food restaurant meals rose from 20 meals per year in 1970 to 90 meals per year in 2000, representing a 350% increase. Similarly, sit-down restaurant meals increased from 10 meals per year to 50 meals per year, a 400% increase. Although fast food restaurants had more meals overall, sit-down restaurants experienced a greater percentage increase.

In summary, there was a clear trend towards eating out more frequently, with families allocating a larger proportion of their food budget to restaurant meals, particularly fast food restaurants.

## 范文解析

### 结构分析
1. **开头段（Introduction）**: 改写题目，说明图表内容
2. **主体段1**: 描述饼图的变化
3. **主体段2**: 描述线图的变化，并进行比较
4. **结尾段（可选）**: 总结主要趋势

### 语言要点

#### 描述变化的词汇
- **增加**: increase, rise, grow, climb
- **减少**: decrease, decline, drop, fall
- **显著变化**: dramatic, significant, substantial, considerable
- **轻微变化**: slight, modest, gradual

#### 描述比例的词汇
- proportion, percentage, share
- allocate to, spend on
- account for, make up

#### 比较的词汇
- similarly, likewise, in contrast, however
- compared to, in comparison with
- whereas, while

## 常见错误

1. **不要添加个人观点**: Task 1只需要描述数据，不需要解释原因或给出建议
2. **不要遗漏主要特征**: 必须描述所有图表的主要信息
3. **注意时态**: 描述过去的数据用过去时，描述趋势用现在时
4. **数据准确性**: 确保引用的数据准确

## 学习要点

1. **开头段写法**: 改写题目，不要直接照抄
2. **主体段组织**: 可以按图表分组，也可以按时间分组
3. **数据引用**: 选择关键数据进行描述，不需要描述所有细节
4. **比较技巧**: 找出数据之间的关联和差异
