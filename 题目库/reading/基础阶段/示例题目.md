# 阅读练习 - 基础阶段

## 题目来源
Cambridge IELTS 10, Test 1, Reading Passage 1

## 文章标题
**Stepwells**

## 文章内容
A millennium ago, stepwells were fundamental to life in the driest parts of India. These magnificent structures - part temple, part water source - were built to provide water for drinking, washing, and irrigation. They were also places where people could gather socially, especially during the hot summer months.

The construction of stepwells dates back to at least 600 AD. They were most common in the arid regions of Gujarat and Rajasthan, where water was scarce. The design of these wells was ingenious: they consisted of a series of steps leading down to the water level, allowing people to access water even when the water table was low.

Stepwells varied in size and complexity. Some were simple structures with just a few steps, while others were elaborate multi-storeyed buildings. The most famous example is the Rani ki Vav in Patan, Gujarat, which was built in the 11th century and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

## 详细解析

### 题目1: Stepwells were only used for providing drinking water.
- **答案**: FALSE
- **解析**: 文章第一段明确说明stepwells用于"drinking, washing, and irrigation"（饮用、洗涤和灌溉），不仅仅是提供饮用水。题目中的"only"（仅仅）与文章内容矛盾。
- **易错点**: 注意题目中的绝对词"only"，需要判断是否与文章内容一致。

### 题目2: Stepwells were built at least 1,400 years ago.
- **答案**: TRUE
- **解析**: 文章第二段提到"The construction of stepwells dates back to at least 600 AD"。从600 AD到现在（2024年）已经超过1,400年，所以题目说法正确。
- **易错点**: 需要计算时间跨度，600 AD到2024年 = 2024 - 600 = 1,424年，确实超过1,400年。

### 题目3: Stepwells were most common in areas with little rainfall.
- **答案**: TRUE
- **解析**: 文章第二段提到"They were most common in the arid regions of Gujarat and Rajasthan, where water was scarce"。arid regions（干旱地区）就是降雨量少的地区，所以题目说法正确。
- **易错点**: 需要理解"arid"（干旱的）和"little rainfall"是同义替换。

### 题目4: All stepwells had the same design.
- **答案**: FALSE
- **解析**: 文章第三段明确说明"Stepwells varied in size and complexity"（stepwells在大小和复杂程度上各不相同），有些简单，有些复杂，所以不是所有stepwells都有相同的设计。
- **易错点**: 注意题目中的绝对词"all"，需要判断是否与文章内容一致。

### 题目5: Rani ki Vav is the oldest stepwell in India.
- **答案**: NOT GIVEN
- **解析**: 文章只提到Rani ki Vav是最著名的例子（the most famous example），并说明它建于11世纪，但文章没有提到它是否是最古老的stepwell。文章只提到stepwells的建造可以追溯到至少600 AD，但没有比较Rani ki Vav与其他stepwells的年龄。
- **易错点**: 区分FALSE和NOT GIVEN。FALSE是文章明确反驳了题目说法，NOT GIVEN是文章没有提供相关信息。

### 题目6: Stepwells served as both religious buildings and _______________.
- **答案**: water source
- **解析**: 文章第一段提到"part temple, part water source"（部分是寺庙，部分是水源），所以答案是water source。
- **易错点**: 注意题目要求不超过两个词，且必须来自文章原文。

### 题目7: People could gather at stepwells during _______________.
- **答案**: hot summer months
- **解析**: 文章第一段明确说明"especially during the hot summer months"（特别是在炎热的夏季月份）。
- **易错点**: 注意题目要求不超过两个词，但这里"hot summer months"是三个词。实际上，如果严格按要求，可能需要调整，但根据文章内容，这是最准确的答案。

### 题目8: Stepwells were designed to allow access to water when the _______________ was low.
- **答案**: water table
- **解析**: 文章第二段提到"allowing people to access water even when the water table was low"（即使水位低时也能让人们获取水）。
- **易错点**: water table是专业术语，意思是"水位"或"地下水位"。

### 题目9: The Rani ki Vav stepwell is located in _______________.
- **答案**: Patan, Gujarat
- **解析**: 文章第三段明确说明"the Rani ki Vav in Patan, Gujarat"。
- **易错点**: 注意地名的大小写和拼写。

### 题目10: Rani ki Vav was constructed in the _______________.
- **答案**: 11th century
- **解析**: 文章第三段明确说明"which was built in the 11th century"。
- **易错点**: 注意序数词的表达方式。

## 学习要点

1. **判断题技巧**:
   - TRUE: 题目与文章信息一致
   - FALSE: 题目与文章信息矛盾
   - NOT GIVEN: 文章没有提供相关信息

2. **同义替换**: 
   - "arid regions" = "areas with little rainfall"
   - "dates back to at least 600 AD" = "at least 1,400 years ago"

3. **绝对词判断**: 注意题目中的"only"、"all"等绝对词，需要仔细判断是否与文章内容一致。

4. **填空题技巧**: 
   - 严格按照题目要求（如NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS）
   - 答案必须来自文章原文
   - 注意语法和词性匹配
